Making Sandwich

Procedure

  1. Take two slices of bread
  2. Prepare a salad stuffs
  3. Add mayonise onto the layer of bread
  4. Add the stuffs prepared
  5. add some tomato sauce with origano sprinkles
  6. toast the bread in sandwich maker and serve happily

Ingredients

  • Raw vegetable mix
  • tomato sauce
  • mayonise and origano flakes
  • bread slices
